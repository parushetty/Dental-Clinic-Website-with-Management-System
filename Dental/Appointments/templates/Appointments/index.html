    ABOUT US!

    Welcome to Shrine Dental Care, a dedicated and independent dental practice committed to delivering top-tier dental care with affordability, all within a welcoming and tranquil atmosphere. Our unwavering passion for providing exceptional dental services is rooted in our patient-centered approach.

    In our effort to make your dental visits as convenient as possible, we offer flexible operating hours. Transparency is a cornerstone of our practice, and we ensure you are fully informed about the pricing of your treatment, the expected number of visits, the duration of each appointment, and the comprehensive treatment plan before any dental procedures commence.

    Our team of dentists at Shrine Dental Care shares a commitment to ongoing dental education, participating in courses to enhance their skills both locally and internationally.

    Our relentless pursuit of professional excellence, coupled with investments in cutting-edge technology, has garnered accolades from our patients. These efforts have solidified our position as a leading private dental clinic in Pondicherry, dedicated to providing you with the highest standard of dental care. We take pride in being your trusted dental clinic in Pondicherry and your go-to dentist in Pondicherry. Your oral health is our priority, and we look forward to welcoming you to Shrine Dental Care, where we place your well-being at the heart of everything we do.

    Dental Fillings

    Dental fillings are composed of various materials, including metals, plastics, glass, or other substances, used to repair and restore teeth, either individually or in combinations.

    Root canal Treatment

    The Root Canal procedure entails the removal of pulp to address decay or infection within a tooth

    Dental crown

    A dental crown is a tooth-shaped cap used to restore and strengthen teeth that are decayed, fractured, weakened, or have undergone significant wear.
